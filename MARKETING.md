
### Detailed Description:
```
AutoApply AI Chrome Extension lets you apply to jobs directly from hh.ru and staff.am without leaving the page.

FEATURES:
- Save any job with one click
- Quick Apply with AI-generated cover letter
- See your application stats (applied, saved, interviews)
- Quick navigation to Dashboard, Tracker, Resume, and Job Agent

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Browse jobs on hh.ru or staff.am
2. Click "Save Job" or "Quick Apply with AI" on any job listing
3. AI generates a personalized cover letter and submits your application
4. Track everything in your AutoApply AI dashboard

REQUIRES:
- Free AutoApply AI account (autoapply-ai-vert.vercel.app)

The extension injects Save and Quick Apply buttons directly on job pages, so you never need to leave the site.

Works on: hh.ru, staff.am
```
